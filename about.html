About Amazon

Who We Are

Amazon is a global technology company that has revolutionized the way people shop, read, and interact with digital services. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, Amazon started as an online bookstore and has since evolved into one of the world’s most customer-centric companies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be Earth's most customer-centric company. We strive to offer customers the lowest possible prices, the best available selection, and the utmost convenience.

What We Do

From e-commerce and cloud computing to artificial intelligence and streaming, Amazon leads innovation across multiple industries. Our products and services include:

  • Amazon.com – online retail marketplace
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) – cloud solutions
  • Alexa – voice assistant technology
  • Kindle – digital reading platform
  • Prime Video – digital entertainment
  • Amazon Devices – Echo, Fire TV, and more

Our Global Impact

With operations in over 20 countries, Amazon employs over 1.5 million people globally. We are committed to sustainable practices, inclusive workplace culture, and innovation that makes life easier for customers and communities.

Our Values

We believe in:

  • Customer obsession over competitor focus
  • Long-term thinking
  • Operational excellence
  • Innovation and simplification
